What is ListLink?

ListLink is a Stremio add-on that automatically adds movies and shows you watch to your Trakt.tv and MDBList lists.

How it works: When browsing content in Stremio, you'll see "Add to List" options in the streams menu. Click any option to instantly add that content to your selected lists.

Connect both services or just one - it's up to you! You can use Trakt only, MDBList only, or both together.
